<p><p>The organic skim milk powder market is primarily divided into two types: regular and instant. Regular skim milk powder is produced by removing fat from pasteurized milk, resulting in a versatile ingredient used in baking, cooking, and beverages. Instant skim milk powder, on the other hand, undergoes a process that allows it to dissolve quickly in water, making it convenient for consumers seeking quick and easy preparation. Both types cater to health-conscious individuals looking for organic dairy alternatives.</p></p>
