<p><p>The window blinds market can be categorized into two main types: manual and electric window blinds. Manual window blinds are operated by hand, allowing users to control light and privacy easily with cords or wands. They are often more affordable and simpler to install. In contrast, electric window blinds offer automated control through remote or smart home integration, providing convenience and enhanced functionality.
