Fully automatic systems handle the entire molding process with minimal human intervention, enhancing efficiency and consistency. Semi-automatic systems require some manual input, striking a balance between automation and flexibility for varied production needs. Manual systems depend on human operation for most tasks, offering lower initial costs but potentially reduced throughput and consistency.
