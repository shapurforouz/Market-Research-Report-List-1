<p><p>The allspice market consists of three primary types: ground allspice, whole allspice, and rub allspice. Whole allspice, with its strong aroma and flavor, is favored for pickling and slow-cooking to infuse dishes gradually. Rub allspice combines allspice with other spices to create a seasoning mix for meats and vegetables, enhancing the complexity of flavors in barbecues and roasts, catering to diverse culinary needs.</p></p>
