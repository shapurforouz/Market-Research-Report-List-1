<p><p>The Dynamic Water Cutting Machine market encompasses two primary types: Sandless Cutting and Abrasive Cutting. Sandless Cutting utilizes high-pressure water jets to cut materials without the addition of abrasives, making it ideal for softer materials and resulting in minimal waste and a clean finish. In contrast, Abrasive Cutting integrates abrasives into the water jet stream, enhancing its ability to slice through harder materials. This method is prevalent in industrial applications requiring precision and efficiency in cutting tough substances.</p></p>
