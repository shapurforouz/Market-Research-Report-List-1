<p><p>The Blood Transport Boxes market is categorized into two main types: Passive Cooling and Active Cooling Blood Transportation Boxes. Passive cooling boxes rely on insulation and gel packs to maintain temperature, effectively preserving blood products during transport without external energy. In contrast, active cooling boxes utilize refrigeration systems to actively regulate temperature, ensuring precise climate control for sensitive blood components. Both types are essential for safe and effective blood transportation, catering to different logistical needs and temperature requirements.</p></p>
