<p><p>The Ultraviolet LED (UV-LED) market is categorized into three primary types: UV-A LED, UV-B LED, and UV-C LED, along with other variations. UV-A LEDs emit longer wavelengths and are primarily used for applications such as curing and blacklighting. UV-B LEDs have shorter wavelengths and are employed in medical therapies and certain sterilization processes. UV-C LEDs emit even shorter wavelengths, targeting disinfection and germicidal applications. The "Other" category includes specialized UV-LEDs for niche industrial and scientific uses.</p></p>
