<p><p>The four-way valve market comprises three primary types: ball valves, piston valves, and other types. Ball valves facilitate efficient flow control with quick operation and low pressure drop, ideal for on/off applications. Piston valves, utilizing a moving piston to regulate flow, provide precise control and are suited for high-pressure systems. Other types may include diaphragm and globe valves, offering specialized functions for diverse industrial applications. Each valve type caters to specific operational needs in fluid management systems.</p></p>
