<p><p>The volumetric flowmeter market includes various types designed for specific applications. Oval gear flow meters measure flow through rotating gears, while scraper flowmeters utilize a blade for particle detection. Dual rotameters provide redundancy for accuracy, and rotary piston flow meters use a rotating piston to measure flow. Reciprocating piston flow meters operate using back-and-forth piston movement. Disc flowmeters use a disc mechanism, while liquid sealed drum type gas flow meters measure gas flow in liquids. Wet gas meters are tailored for measuring gas mixed with liquids.</p></p>
