<p><strong>The Mask Inspection & Review Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Die to Die (DD) Method</li><li>Die to Database (DB) Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Mask Inspection & Review Equipment market encompasses advanced systems used in semiconductor manufacturing to ensure mask quality. The Die to Die (DD) method focuses on comparing the patterns within individual die on a mask to detect defects directly, enhancing accuracy in yield prediction. Conversely, the Die to Database (DB) method involves comparing die patterns against a pre-established database of defect-free designs, facilitating more efficient identification of anomalies. Both methods are crucial in maintaining the integrity of semiconductor production processes.</p></p>
