<p><p>The Polyoxymethylene (POM) resin market is divided into two main types: copolymer and homopolymer. Homopolymer POM is known for its high rigidity and stability, making it suitable for engineering applications requiring precision and durability. In contrast, copolymer POM offers enhanced toughness and lower susceptibility to stress cracking, ideal for applications where flexibility and impact resistance are essential. Both types serve diverse industries, including automotive, electronics, and consumer goods, catering to specific performance requirements and application needs.</p></p>
