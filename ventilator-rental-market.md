<p><p>The ventilator rental market comprises several types, including axial flow, centrifugal, and others. Axial flow ventilators move air parallel to the fan axis, providing efficient airflow for ventilating spaces. Centrifugal ventilators, on the other hand, use a rotating mechanism to draw air in at an angle and expel it at a right angle, offering high pressure and airflow for larger applications. Other types may encompass hybrid models and specialized devices designed for specific medical or industrial needs, enhancing versatility in ventilation solutions.</p></p>
