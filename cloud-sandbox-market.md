<p><p>The Cloud Sandbox market consists of two main types: Standalone Sandboxes and Integrated Sandboxes. Standalone Sandboxes operate independently, providing a secure environment for testing applications without affecting production systems. They are ideal for developers needing a dedicated space for experimentation. Integrated Sandboxes, on the other hand, work within existing cloud infrastructure, allowing seamless connection to other services and facilitating collaborative development. This integration enhances efficiency, enabling teams to test in a more synchronized and streamlined manner.</p></p>
