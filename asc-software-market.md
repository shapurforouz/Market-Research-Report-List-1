<p><p>The ASC software market is divided into two main types: cloud-based and on-premise ambulatory software. Cloud-based ambulatory software offers flexibility and accessibility, allowing healthcare providers to access patient data and systems from any location with internet connectivity. This model often features lower upfront costs and scalable solutions. Both options serve to enhance operational efficiency in ambulatory care settings.</p></p>
