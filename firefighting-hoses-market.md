<p><p>The firefighting hoses market primarily includes lined fire hoses and leaky fire hoses. Lined fire hoses are designed with internal linings that enhance durability and reduce friction, allowing for efficient water flow and improved longevity under high-pressure conditions. Conversely, leaky fire hoses feature intentional perforations, enabling controlled water discharge in specific applications, such as training or controlled burns. Both types play crucial roles in firefighting operations, addressing varying needs in water delivery and fire suppression tactics.</p></p>
