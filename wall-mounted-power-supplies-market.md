<p><p>The wall-mounted power supplies market comprises several types based on output configurations. Single output supplies deliver power through one output channel, typically serving basic devices. Dual tracking output supplies provide two outputs that vary in voltage, often used for testing applications. Dual isolated output supplies offer two independently isolated outputs, ensuring safety for sensitive equipment. Triple isolated output units provide three independent outputs, catering to more complex systems. Additionally, the "others" category includes specialized configurations designed for unique applications.</p></p>
