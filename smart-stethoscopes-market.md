<p><p>The smart stethoscope market is divided into two main types: wireless stethoscopes and wired stethoscopes. Wireless stethoscopes utilize Bluetooth or Wi-Fi technology to transmit auscultation sounds to smartphones or computers, enhancing portability and data accessibility for healthcare professionals. In contrast, wired stethoscopes connect directly to devices through cables, offering reliable sound quality without the need for batteries or charging. Both types aim to improve patient care by providing advanced diagnostic capabilities and facilitating remote monitoring and consultation.</p></p>
