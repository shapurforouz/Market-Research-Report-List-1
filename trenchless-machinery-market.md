<p><p>The trenchless machinery market features various types, including Horizontal Directional Drilling (HDD) machines and pipe jacking machines. HDD machines are utilized for installing underground utilities without disturbing surface activities, making them ideal for urban areas. Conversely, pipe jacking machines involve pushing pipes through the ground using hydraulic jacks, suitable for replacing existing pipelines. Both technologies reduce environmental impact and excavation costs, making trenchless methods increasingly popular for infrastructure development and maintenance projects worldwide.</p></p>
