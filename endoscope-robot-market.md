<p><ul><li>Multi-Hole Mirror Robot</li><li>Single-Hole Mirror Robot</li><li>No-Hole Mirror Robot</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The endoscope robot market is segmented into three main types: Multi-Hole Mirror Robots, Single-Hole Mirror Robots, and No-Hole Mirror Robots. Multi-Hole Mirror Robots utilize multiple entry points for enhanced maneuverability and visualization. Single-Hole Mirror Robots are designed for procedures requiring only one entry point, simplifying access while maintaining effectiveness. No-Hole Mirror Robots offer a non-invasive approach, eliminating the need for any incisions, making them ideal for delicate procedures. Each type caters to specific surgical needs, facilitating advancements in minimally invasive techniques.</p></p>
