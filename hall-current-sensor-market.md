<p><p>The Hall Current Sensor market is divided into two main types: analog output Hall sensors and digital output Hall sensors. Analog output Hall sensors provide a continuous voltage signal proportional to the magnetic field, making them suitable for precise current measurements. In contrast, digital output Hall sensors convert the magnetic field into discrete signals, offering simpler interface and processing capabilities for smart applications. Both types cater to various industries, including automotive, industrial, and consumer electronics, ensuring accurate current monitoring and control.</p></p>
