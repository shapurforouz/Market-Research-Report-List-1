<p><p>The Optically Pumped Magnetoencephalography (OPM-MEG) System is an advanced neuroimaging technology that measures magnetic fields produced by neuronal activity in the brain. Unlike traditional MEG systems, which rely on superconducting materials, OPM-MEG employs optically pumped sensors that enable greater sensitivity and a more compact design. This technology offers improved spatial resolution and the ability to conduct more comfortable, real-time imaging, making it invaluable for both research and clinical applications in neurology and cognitive science.</p><p>The OPM-MEG market is witnessing significant growth due to increasing demand for non-invasive brain monitoring techniques, rising prevalence of neurological disorders, and advancements in sensor technology. Key trends driving this market include the integration of machine learning algorithms for data analysis, improving accuracy of brain mapping, and the expansion of applications beyond clinical use, such as in cognitive neuroscience and brain-computer interfaces. The OPM-MEG System Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7% during the forecast period, reflecting the ongoing innovation and investment in neuroimaging technologies aimed at enhancing patient care and understanding of brain functions.</p></p>
