<p><p>The UV phototherapy light market can be divided into two main types: broadband UV and narrowband UV. Broadband UV devices emit a wide spectrum of ultraviolet light, making them effective for treating various skin conditions but often associated with more side effects. In contrast, narrowband UV devices target a specific range of wavelengths, primarily UVB, allowing for more precise treatments with fewer side effects, enhancing their efficacy for conditions such as psoriasis and eczema. Each type serves distinct therapeutic needs.</p></p>
