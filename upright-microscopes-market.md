<p><p>The upright microscopes market encompasses three primary types: optical, electron, and scanning probe microscopes. Optical microscopes use visible light and optics to magnify samples for basic observation in biological and material sciences. Electron microscopes employ electrons for highly detailed imaging at the nano scale, making them vital for advanced research in materials and biology. Scanning probe microscopes utilize a probe to scan the surface at atomic resolution, offering insights into surface properties and structures at an unprecedented level of detail.</p></p>
