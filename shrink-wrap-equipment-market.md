<p><ul><li>PE Film Shrink Machine</li><li>POF Film Shrink Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The shrink wrap equipment market primarily consists of two main types: PE film shrink machines and POF film shrink machines. PE film shrink machines utilize polyethylene film, known for its strength and affordability, making them ideal for heavy-duty applications. In contrast, POF film shrink machines use polyolefin film, which offers superior clarity and flexibility, suitable for retail packaging and visually appealing products. Both types are vital in automating packaging processes, enhancing efficiency and product presentation across various industries.</p></p>
