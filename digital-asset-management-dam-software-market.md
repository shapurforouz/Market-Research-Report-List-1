<p><p>Digital Asset Management (DAM) software is categorized into two main market types: Cloud and On-Premises. Cloud-based DAM solutions provide remote access to assets via the internet, offering flexibility, scalability, and lower upfront costs, making them ideal for organizations that require mobility and collaboration. In contrast, On-Premises DAM software is installed directly on an organization's servers, providing greater control over data security and customization, suitable for companies with specific security requirements or those preferring to maintain their infrastructure.</p></p>
