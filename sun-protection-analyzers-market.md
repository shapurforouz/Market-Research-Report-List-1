<p><ul><li>Sun Protection Factor</li><li>UV Protection Factor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Sun Protection Analyzer market encompasses two primary types: Sun Protection Factor (SPF) and Ultraviolet Protection Factor (UPF). SPF measures the effectiveness of sunscreen in preventing UVB rays from causing skin burns, providing a numerical rating to indicate protection levels. In contrast, UPF assesses the UV protection offered by clothing and fabrics against both UVA and UVB rays. Both metrics are essential for consumers seeking effective sun protection solutions, driving demand for innovative products and testing technologies in the market.</p></p>
