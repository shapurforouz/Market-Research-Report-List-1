<p><p>The Sample Transfer System market is divided into two main types: Cryogenic Sample Transfer Systems and Room Temperature Sample Transfer Systems. Cryogenic systems are designed to handle samples at extremely low temperatures, essential for preserving the integrity of sensitive materials such as biological specimens and superconductors. In contrast, Room Temperature Sample Transfer Systems operate at ambient conditions, suitable for routine laboratory samples and applications where temperature control is less critical. Each type caters to specific industry needs and safety requirements.</p></p>
