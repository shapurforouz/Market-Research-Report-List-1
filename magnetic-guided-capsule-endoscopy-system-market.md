<p><p>The Magnetic-Guided Capsule Endoscopy System market comprises two primary types: Rotating Magnetic Field and Static Magnetic Field systems. Rotating Magnetic Field systems utilize dynamically changing magnetic fields to steer the capsule through the gastrointestinal tract, enhancing visualization and control. In contrast, Static Magnetic Field systems employ fixed magnetic fields for navigation, ensuring consistent positioning. Both types aim to improve diagnostic accuracy and patient outcomes by providing non-invasive examination options in gastroenterology, catering to diverse clinical needs and technological advancements.</p></p>
