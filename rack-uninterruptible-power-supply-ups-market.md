<p><p>The Rack Uninterruptible Power Supply (UPS) market comprises three main types: Static UPS, Dynamic UPS, and Hybrid UPS. Static UPS systems utilize batteries to provide backup power, ensuring a stable output. Dynamic UPS systems, on the other hand, use rotating machinery to offer both power backup and conditioning, resulting in higher efficiency and responsiveness. Hybrid UPS combines both static and dynamic technologies, optimizing performance and reliability while minimizing downtime, catering to various power requirements in data centers and critical infrastructure.</p></p>
