<p><strong>The Vascular Allografts Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cortical Allograft</li><li>Cancellous Allograft</li><li>Corticocancellous Allograft</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vascular allografts market comprises three primary types: cortical allografts, cancellous allografts, and corticocancellous allografts. Cortical allografts, derived from the dense outer layer of bone, provide structural support and strength, making them ideal for load-bearing applications. Cancellous allografts, sourced from the spongy interior bone, are favored for their excellent biological properties and ability to promote healing. Corticocancellous allografts combine features of both types, providing structural stability along with osteogenic potential, making them versatile for various surgical procedures.</p></p>
