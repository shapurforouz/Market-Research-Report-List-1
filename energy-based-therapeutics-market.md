<p><p>Energy-based therapeutics encompass various modalities for treating medical conditions through specific energy applications. Microwave therapies utilize electromagnetic radiation to heat tissues, promoting healing. Radiofrequency techniques apply electrical currents to target tissue, often for pain management. Hydro-mechanical therapies use water pressure for therapeutic effects. Cryotherapy employs extreme cold to reduce inflammation and pain. Thermal therapies involve heat application for pain relief and tissue healing. Ultrasound therapies utilize sound waves for diagnostic and therapeutic purposes, enhancing tissue repair and alleviating pain.</p></p>
