<p><p>The truck-mounted drilling rig market encompasses three main types: hydraulic pressure, pneumatic, and power-driven rigs. Hydraulic pressure rigs utilize hydraulic systems to generate high force, making them efficient for various drilling tasks. Pneumatic rigs rely on compressed air to facilitate drilling, ideal for softer materials and quick operations. Power-driven rigs depend on mechanical engines or motors, offering versatility in power sources. Each type is designed to suit specific geological conditions and operational requirements, enhancing drilling efficiency and effectiveness.</p></p>
