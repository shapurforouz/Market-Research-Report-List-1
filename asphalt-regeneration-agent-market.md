<p><strong>The Asphalt Regeneration Agent Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Heat-In-Place Regeneration</li><li>Plant Mix Hot Regeneration</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The asphalt regeneration agent market primarily encompasses two types: Heat-In-Place Regeneration and Plant Mix Hot Regeneration. Heat-In-Place Regeneration involves reheating existing asphalt surfaces to restore their properties and improve performance, typically utilized in surface treatments. In contrast, Plant Mix Hot Regeneration entails the incorporation of regeneration agents in an asphalt plant, blending them with new materials to produce high-quality hot mix asphalt. Both methods aim to extend the lifecycle of road surfaces and enhance sustainability in pavement maintenance.</p></p>
