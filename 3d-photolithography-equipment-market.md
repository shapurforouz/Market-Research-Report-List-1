<p><ul><li>Ultraviolet (UV) Laser</li><li>Extreme Ultraviolet Laser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 3D photolithography equipment market includes two main types: Ultraviolet (UV) lasers and Extreme Ultraviolet (EUV) lasers. UV lasers utilize wavelengths typically ranging from 200 to 400 nm, making them suitable for high-resolution patterning in semiconductor manufacturing and microfabrication. In contrast, EUV lasers operate at shorter wavelengths around 13.5 nm, enabling even finer patterning for advanced processes in cutting-edge semiconductor technologies. Both types are essential for producing intricate designs for microchips and other applications in electronics and materials science.</p></p>
