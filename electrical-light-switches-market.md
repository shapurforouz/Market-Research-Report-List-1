<p><ul><li>Rheostat</li><li>Coil-rotation transformer</li><li>Autotransformer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electrical light switches market includes various types such as rheostats, coil-rotation transformers, and autotransformers. Rheostats allow users to adjust resistance and control light intensity. Coil-rotation transformers, often used in dimming applications, modify output voltage through mechanical rotation of coils. Autotransformers efficiently regulate voltage levels by sharing primary and secondary windings, making them suitable for varying power requirements. Each type serves specific applications in lighting control, with unique operational features to enhance user experience and energy efficiency.</p></p>
