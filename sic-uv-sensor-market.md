<p><p>The SiC UV sensor market is segmented into three main types: UVA, UVB, and UVC sensors. UVB sensors measure mid-range UV radiation, crucial for monitoring skin damage and UV exposure. UVC sensors target short-wave UV radiation, essential for disinfection processes and water treatment. Each sensor type leverages silicon carbide (SiC) technology for enhanced sensitivity, durability, and performance in diverse environmental conditions.</p></p>
